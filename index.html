How Arena Banafix works

  • Pick up to 4 Banafixes from your collection.
  • Only Banafixes with daily arena uses left can be selected.
  • Most fights have 3 enemies, sometimes 2 on early stages, and rarely 4.
  • Enemy Banafixes get better rarity as stages go up.
  • Clearing a stage gives XP, and the full run gives a final bonus at the end.
  • Daily arena uses reset at 1:00.
